Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

💡 Short version: We offer a 7-day refund on all plans if you're not satisfied. No questions asked.

1. Our Refund Guarantee

At NexBot, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with our service for any reason, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase.

2. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request the refund within 7 days of your subscription start date
  • You have not violated our Terms and Conditions
  • This is your first refund request for this account

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at support@nexchatai.netlify.app with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your Razorpay payment ID
  • Reason for refund (optional but helpful)

We will process your refund within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Refund requests made after 7 days of purchase
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Service
  • Renewal charges (we send reminder emails before renewal)
  • Partial month usage — we do not prorate refunds

5. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund. You will continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@nexchatai.netlify.app. We typically respond within 24 hours.
