FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need coding skills to set up NexBot? +
No coding at all! Just fill in a form with your business info and FAQs, pick your colors, and copy one line of code into your website. Takes under 10 minutes.
What websites does NexBot work on? +
NexBot works on any website — Shopify, WordPress, WooCommerce, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, custom HTML sites, and React/Next.js apps. One line of code and you're done.
Can I try it before paying? +
Yes! Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You only pay if you love it.
How does the bot know about my business? +
You train it! In the setup form, you describe what your business does and list your common FAQs. The AI uses this information to answer customer questions accurately.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel anytime from your account with one click. We don't make canceling difficult.
What languages does NexBot support? +
English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Portuguese, and Japanese. The bot auto-detects what language your customer writes in and replies in the same language.
